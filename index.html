Event Schedule

  • Morning Session

  • Morning Session
    08:00 - 10:00 Check-in & Breakfast
    10:00 - 10:15 Event Opening
    10:15 - 10:45 Growth Mindset

    Our success is fostered or hindered depending on the way we understand our intelligence and abilities. we will discuss the mindset concepts of fixed and growth, and how avoiding challenges (fixed mindset) when things get tough is like driving a nail in the coffin of your own opportunities for success. This talk should be based on psychology & Science-based evidence. Takeaway: There are clear actions we can all take to establish a growth-based mindset and enable our success.

    Speaker : Ikdem Ben Mbarek
    10:45 - 11:15 Psychology of the mind

    Whenever we discuss ideas with people we tend to use our personal logic to give arguments. In fact, these arguments may lack a truth to them. Maybe we need to think again on how to provide arguments and actually base them on credible logic.

    Speaker : Houda Sghaier Benhoula
    11:15 - 11:45 Yutna Band
    11:45 - 12:15 Medicine from another angle

    Taking risks is part of our lives and no one can deny it. However, taking risks to save lives is something which is exceptional and difficult. Doctors have to put their skills to the test quite often in order to manage their situation. Doctors are always given a choice between saving a life and sticking to the rules.

    Speaker : Faouzi Drissi
    12:15 - 12:30 TED Talk Video 1
    12:30 - 13:00 Magic Show
    13:00 Lunch Time
  • Afternoon Session
    14:00 - 14:30 Social Entrepreneurship

    In this talk, we want to urge our elite Jendoubian audience to be brave enough to build purpose-driven enterprises that address local and national problems. we will describe the ways in which social entrepreneurship differs from more traditional entrepreneurship, and from charity work, along with the ways in which purpose-driven entrepreneurialism, “a thriving, new sector,” can lead to lasting, positive change at the local and global level. this talk should focus mainly on the idea, not the speaker, and definitely not his startup/company. Takeaway: These social entrepreneurs, whether they want to change locally or nationally, they’re not professors in social impact or all business school graduates. They’re people like you and me. They’re people who are frustrated and want to make a change.

    Speaker : Soumaya Atoui
    14:30 - 15:00 Understanding love

    The way we manifest our love for another person is almost always based on our understanding of love itself. However, love can be mistaken for affection or attraction and so on. Can people actually distinguish between their feelings? How can we show and live love itself if we have the wrong understanding of it.

    Speaker : Mariem Oueslati
    15:00 - 15:30 Yutna Band
    15:30 - 16:00 Investing in your region

    This talk is basically about opportunities in Jendouba. Tech startups are all the rage these days. this is because the Internet democratised technology and everyone practically has the same access to the same information. But, what about investing in something original? Something only a few people are experts at : your region. We ask a very simple question : what treasures can we uncover investing in our region?

Frequently asked questions

Some frequently asked questions for you.

Is this event free ?

Our event is completely FREE!
BUT you have to buy a ticket because we have limited seats. Tickets will be available online before few days from the kick off. If you don't show up after closing the checkin, your ticket will pass automatically to the next participant on our waiting list.

Is it age restricted ?

Absolutely not! The event is open for everybody. Be there with your parents or your friends and watch a conference that may change your perspective or your point of view about a certain topic.

Should I bring my own food/drinks ?

No, there will be two coffee breaks during the event. There will be plenty of food and drinks to cover the needs of everyone. Just watch the conference and enjoy!
